Vanessa Kernweis
As a graduate of the Digital Worlds Institute at the University of Florida, my education
helped me to turn my passion for the arts into practical knowledge and allowed me to
hone the skills I have been practicing for many years.

I have experience with the Adobe Suite, Clip Studio Paint, Toon Boom, Blender, Unity, and
the C++/ C# programming languages. I am familiar with the production pipeline and am
skilled at communicating with people of all different backgrounds and specialties. I am a
competent, creative individual who provides experience and a keen eye for design to any
project team.

While my studies at the University of Florida were focused on digital art and design, I have
always had an interest in language and writing. I find joy in learning new concepts and
applying them to my work.
